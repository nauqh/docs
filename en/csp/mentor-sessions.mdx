Weekly sessions with your mentor are essential to your learning journey at CoderSchool. You might not see the interface below the first time you sign in to CoderSchool Platform. Once our team finds a suitable mentor for you, you will be able to see the details of your upcoming mentor sessions.

![Mentor sessions overview](/imgs/mentor-sessions/image0.png)

## Overview

### Learning progress

![Mentor sessions learning progress](/imgs/mentor-sessions/image1.png)

At the top of the screen, you can find a summary of your learning progress:

1. Your overall progress in the course
2. The average score of all of your exams and assignments
3. The course module you are currently at
4. The next unit/section you will learn

### Mentor session list

![Mentor sessions mentor session list](/imgs/mentor-sessions/image2.png)

Here you can see all of your upcoming and past mentor sessions:

1. The total number of mentor sessions
2. Tab containing upcoming sessions
3. Tab containing past sessions
4. Date and time of a session
5. Attendance status of the session

### Activity log

![Mentor sessions activity log](/imgs/mentor-sessions/image3.png)

The Activity Log is where you can find all the previous activities related to your mentor sessions, such as sessions being canceled or rescheduled.

### Attendance status

![Mentor sessions attendance status](/imgs/mentor-sessions/image4.png)

The status of any mentor session can be found on the icons on the far left side. You can also hover on the icon to see its corresponding status name.

![Mentor sessions joining a mentor session](/imgs/mentor-sessions/image5.png)

## Joining a mentor session

![Mentor sessions joining a mentor session](/imgs/mentor-sessions/image6.png)

Ten minutes before a session starts, and while it is happening, the UI will display a **Join now** button. Clicking it will generate and direct you to the Google Meet link for your mentor session.

<Warning>
  **Important:** You must click the **Join now** button for your attendance to be recorded. If you join your mentor session by any other means, you will be marked as **Absent** for that session.
</Warning>

## Writing recaps

![Mentor sessions writing recaps](/imgs/mentor-sessions/image7.png)

Your feedback is extremely important for us to understand your experience during the course so we can provide timely and suitable support.

Writing a recap for your mentor session takes about one minute. Please let us know whether you are happy or upset, excited or struggling.

- The **Write Recap** button appears for ongoing and finished sessions.
- You can view the recaps you have written by clicking the **View recap** button.

![Mentor sessions writing recaps](/imgs/mentor-sessions/image8.png)

## Review a lesson recording

1. Go to `https://platform.coderschool.vn/`, then sign in with the Gmail account you used to register for the course.
2. Click **Plans**, then select **Past** to view your completed sessions and choose the session recording you want to review.

![Mentor sessions reviewing a lesson recording](/imgs/mentor-sessions/image.png)

## Canceling a mentor session

If you have urgent business and cannot attend a mentor session, you can cancel a scheduled or ongoing mentor session.

<Info>
  A finished session cannot be canceled.
</Info>

![Mentor sessions canceling a mentor session](/imgs/mentor-sessions/image9.png)

In the **Notes** section, let your mentor know why you want to cancel the session. The Notes section is now required.

![Mentor sessions canceling a mentor session](/imgs/mentor-sessions/image10.png)

<Tip>
  We encourage you to reschedule sessions instead of canceling. If you and your mentor have agreed on a different meeting time, use the rescheduling option.
</Tip>

## Rescheduling a mentor session

You can request to reschedule a mentor session to another time. There are some constraints:

<Warning>
  - The rescheduled session must be after the previous session and before the next session
  - A session cannot be rescheduled to the past
  - Scheduled and Canceled sessions can be rescheduled. **Canceled by mentor** sessions can be rescheduled from the past to upcoming if the following session has not started
  - Reschedule request expiration: 48 hours after its creation time, or at the proposed start time, whichever is sooner
</Warning>

This action sends a reschedule request to your mentor. They will also be notified by email.

- If your mentor accepts the request, the mentor session will be rescheduled to the proposed date and time
- If your mentor rejects the request, the mentor session start time will not be updated
- The reschedule request will expire at the proposed date and time, so remind your mentor to accept it

![Mentor sessions rescheduling a mentor session](/imgs/mentor-sessions/image11.png)

In the **Notes** section, let your mentor know why you want to reschedule the session.

![Mentor sessions rescheduling a mentor session](/imgs/mentor-sessions/image12.png)

## Responding to a reschedule request

Your mentor can also send you a reschedule request. Once they do, you can accept or reject it by hovering on the reschedule request for that session.

![Mentor sessions responding to a reschedule request](/imgs/mentor-sessions/image13.png)

<Tip>
  Please be sure that you have properly communicated with your mentor about the rescheduled session.
</Tip>

If you decide to reject a request, fill in **Your Reason** so your mentor knows why.

![Mentor sessions responding to a reschedule request](/imgs/mentor-sessions/image14.png)
