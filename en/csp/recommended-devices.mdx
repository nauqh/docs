description: "Recommended devices for learning"
---

To avoid technical issues during class, use a **desktop** or **laptop** when learning.

<Warning>
  Mobile phones and tablets are not recommended for coursework, mentor sessions, exams, or submissions.
</Warning>

## Browser recommendation

Use **Google Chrome** as your primary browser for all learning activities on CoderSchool Platform and LearnWorlds.

## Quick checklist

- Use a desktop or laptop (Windows or macOS)
- Do not use mobile phones or tablets for classes
- Use the latest version of **Google Chrome**
