You can access your user settings by clicking your profile picture in the top right corner, then **Settings**.

## Profile

In the **Profile** tab you can update your:

- Personal information
- Contact information
- Professional information

![User settings profile](/imgs/user-settings/image1.png)

## Availability

The **Availability** tab lets you set the time slots during the week when you are available to meet with your mentor.

<Warning>
  On the Availability page, the Platform always records times in **GMT+7 (Indonesia Time)**. If you are in a different timezone, convert your availability to GMT+7 before entering it. On other pages, the Platform displays content in your local timezone.
</Warning>

Times shown on the Availability page are in **GMT+7 (Indochina Time)**.

![User settings availability](/imgs/user-settings/image2.png)

### Adding a time slot

1. Click the **+** icon next to the day. You can add multiple time slots per day.
2. Select or type the **start** time for the slot.
3. Select or type the **end** time for the slot.
4. Click **Save** when you are done.

## Security

In the **Security** tab you can change your password.
