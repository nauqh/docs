<Warning>
  Completing and passing the Final Project is mandatory for graduation eligibility.
</Warning>

## Important notes

- You must complete the Final Project and pass the presentation round on time.
- Late completion may require an extension and additional fees.
- If you do not graduate on time, your account may be permanently locked.

## Final project workflow

## Phase 1: complete and submit your Final Project

<Info>
  You must complete and submit your project review to your mentor in your last mentor session.
</Info>

### 1) Notify and assign TA

- `Mentor/Learner`: Notify Learner Success (LS) in the Discord group when the learner starts the Final Project.
- `LS`: Check TA workload, then assign a Teaching Assistant (TA) to support the learner.
- `TA`: Align with the learner on a weekly support schedule for the Final Project.

### 2) Final Project approval

- `Mentor`: Within **24 hours** after the last mentor session, the mentor reviews the Final Project.
  - **PASS**: Mentor announces the result in the Discord group.
  - **FAIL**: Mentor informs the TA via Discord so the learner can revise.

<Warning>
  The submission deadline is 24 hours after your last mentor session.
</Warning>

## Phase 2: presentation and grading

### 1) Schedule final presentation

- `TA`: Schedule the Final Presentation with 2 external mentors within **48 hours** after project approval, using Instant Meeting on the platform.
- `Learner`: Present your project within **7 days** after submission.

### 2) Evaluate Final Project

- `Mentor`: Complete evaluation within **24 hours** after the Final Presentation, using the evaluation forms.
- `TA`: Update feedback on LearnWorld and notify the learner via Discord.
- `LS`: Update learner status on the platform after receiving the Grad Check-in email.
- `Learner`: After receiving the result from TA on Discord, claim your online certificate on the learning platform in the `Final Project` section.

## Stay proactive

Track your progress closely and work proactively with your mentor and TA to complete your Final Project on time.
