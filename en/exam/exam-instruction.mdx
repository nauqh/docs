Welcome to the CoderSchool eAssessment Platform, an online examination system designed to test your knowledge and skills in data analysis. This guide helps you understand how to navigate and complete your exam successfully.

<Warning>
  You must complete the exam in **one continuous sitting**. Do not exit or close your browser before finishing.
</Warning>

## Exam structure

Your exam normally includes two main sections:

1. **Multiple-choice questions**
2. **Coding problems**

## Start your exam

1. Click the exam link provided by your instructor to access the exam portal.
2. Carefully read all instructions and exam policies on the screen.
3. When you are ready, click **START EXAM** and enter your email to begin.

## Multiple-choice section

In this section, you select the correct answer from four options.

### Answering questions

1. Read each question carefully before choosing an answer.
2. Select your answer by:
   - Clicking on the option, **or**
   - Using keyboard shortcuts:
     - Press `A`, `B`, `C`, or `D` to select the corresponding option.
     - Press **Enter** to confirm your choice and move to the next question.

![Multiple-choice question example](/imgs/exam/image3.png)

### Navigating between questions

You can use the navigation panel on the right side:

- See how many questions you have answered and how many remain.
- Click any question number to jump directly to that question.
- Questions you have answered are marked with a **green** dot.

![Multiple-choice navigation panel](/imgs/exam/image4.png)

### Viewing images in questions

Some questions include images:

- Click the image (🔍) to view it in full size.
- Scroll up and down to zoom in and out.
- Drag the image to view different parts more closely.

### Complete the multiple-choice section

After you answer all questions, a **Complete** button appears:

- Click **Complete** to move to the next section.
- You can review and change your answers **before** you click **Complete**.

![Complete multiple-choice section](/imgs/exam/image7.png)

## Coding problems section

In this section, you solve practical programming problems in the code editor. Depending on the module, you may be asked to submit:

- An **SQL query**
- A **Python function**
- A **Pandas expression**

### Answering coding problems

1. Read the problem instructions carefully to understand:
   - The requirements
   - Input and output formats
   - Any constraints or edge cases

<Info>
  In some exams, you may be given an ERD or an external notebook. Always read the instructions on the screen carefully to see what resources you can use.
</Info>

2. Write your solution in the code editor.
   - Click **Run** to test your code.
   - Click **Submit** to submit your answer for that problem.
   - Drag the horizontal bar between the **Input** and **Output** sections to resize them.

![Coding problem editor](/imgs/exam/image8.png)

When you have answered all coding problems in this section, click the **Complete** button to continue.

## Finalize your exam submission

After completing all sections, you will see a final review page where you can check your answers.

1. Review all sections to make sure you are happy with your responses.
2. When ready, click **Submit Exam** to finalize your attempt.

Once submitted:

- A confirmation screen appears.
- Your submission is saved locally and a copy is downloaded automatically. Keep this copy for your records.

On the confirmation screen you will see two options:

- **Submit for Grading** — Click this to send your exam for review. Our teaching staff will grade your submission and return the results, usually within **24 hours**.
- **Retake Exam** — Click this if you want to attempt the exam again (only available if your exam settings allow retakes).

![After submit screen](/imgs/exam/aftersubmit1.png)

After you click **Submit for Grading**, a second confirmation appears to show that:

- Your submission has been sent for grading.
- The TA team has been notified.

You can then safely close the exam window.

![Grading confirmation screen](/imgs/exam/aftersubmit2.png)

<Warning>
  Before clicking the final **Submit Exam** button on the review page, double-check your answers. You cannot make any further changes after submitting.
</Warning>

## Technical support

If you encounter technical issues during your exam:

- Try refreshing the page.
- If the problem continues, take a screenshot of your screen and contact the TA team via the **[Discord Question Center](https://discord.com/channels/957854915194126336/1081063200377806899)** for support.
